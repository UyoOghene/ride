About Rume

Rume is a professional designated driver service operating across Canada. Our drivers are fully licensed, insured, and trained to provide you with safe and reliable transportation when you're unable to drive yourself.

We understand that life happens - whether it's a medical procedure, unexpected illness, or a night out with friends. Our mission is to ensure you and your vehicle get home safely, with dignity and professionalism.
