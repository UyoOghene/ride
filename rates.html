Frequently Asked Questions

How is billing calculated?

We charge by the hour with a 2-hour minimum. Billing starts when we arrive at your location and ends when you're safely delivered. Additional mileage beyond the included radius is charged at $2.50/mile.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and corporate accounts. Cash payments are accepted but must be arranged in advance.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance incur no fee. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged 50% of the scheduled service time.
